Welcome to our Packages Page!

The way our packages are set up go hand in hand with our mission to completely personalize your wedding to your needs! Our four packages are based on a points system that takes into consideration the time and work invested for each service, and transforms it into a corresponding point value.

We have four packages:

Sapphire Package

This package is ideal for the client that has almost everything they need finalized for their event minus a few small details. It is most similar to a “Day-of” co-ordination service package where our professional team comes in to execute and showcase all of your ideas and hard work.

Ruby Package

The Ruby package is for clients who have done most of their planning but need someone to step in and help out during the month or so leading up to their event. The goal would be to pick up where you left off in order for you to take the time before your event to focus on your role- enjoying the day and being stress-free. This package allows you to select the specific services you require and leave the organizing details to us.

Emerald Package

Perhaps you have already set a date and maybe even selected a venue for your event, but have yet to select any other vendors- the Emerald Package could be for you! Whatever your needs may be: finding a great entertainment company, selecting a florist who can create what you envision, or finding a photographer that can capture the details you put so much time into, Hand in Hand Weddings & Events can guide you through it. No matter how much planning you have put in to your event, there are still infinite details to attend to and you are going to be busy with other things. Let us take care of the rest.

Diamond Package

This package is designed to cater to the clients that we attend to from the day we are hired to the day we leave your event. From the initial selection of vendors, creation of a guest list, creation of an itinerary, and to the day it is all executed, you need someone available to handle those details. Hand in Hand Weddings & Events will be there to work with you in finalizing details and ensuring your day flows as effortlessly and smoothly as possible. We offer you a peace of mind, enabling you and your guests to enjoy and treasure each and every moment of your event, knowing that experienced people are going hand in hand with you every step of the way.

About Us

Dina Shikhman

Background

I spent my childhood ballroom dancing in spectacular venues, my teenage years singing opera in breathtaking halls and attending friend’s weddings, engagement parties and other wonderful events. I was so captivated by the splendor I came to know, that every part of me craved for it to continue. When I realized that I can bring that beauty and passion to others-Hand in Hand Weddings & Events was born.

It became simple -I have a passion for order and for creating beautiful events. Helping others transform some of their lives’ biggest moments from a dream to a reality became the obvious career path. Since then my team and I commit ourselves to making our client's special days our number one priority.

Company Mission

Our main initiative is to make sure that you and your needs are what drive this process. YOU are what make your event unique, creative and special, and we are here to make sure we showcase that in every way.

We are here to hold your hand throughout the days and months leading up to, and including the day you embark on a journey of a life together going hand in hand with your soul mate.  No matter how big or small your event, we are here to make every detail of your event unforgettable and stress free. From the initial meeting to the big day we are here for you throughout every step of the way!

Initiatives

Although weddings are a huge part of what we do, we are proud to have been a part of other events that hold just as much importance as your wedding day. We’ve had the honour of planning engagement parties, baby showers, birthday parties, corporate events and much more! So please, don’t hesitate to contact us with any inquiries regarding your next event.
